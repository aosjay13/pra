March 12, 2026 Update:

PRA Announces New Jr. Board Members

The Prodigy Racing Association (PRA) is excited to officially welcome five new members to the PRA Jr. Board. As PRA continues to grow, the Jr. Board plays an important role in helping manage league operations, support drivers, and strengthen communication across our series.

Please join us in welcoming the newest members of the PRA Jr. Board:

  • Lilmaytag73
  • DavidN
  • JRaySunshine
  • Spider_Monkey_PU
  • RedlineonCanvas

The Jr. Board serves as a key leadership group within PRA. These members act as the first line of communication between drivers, officials, and the full PRA Board of Directors. They help oversee series operations, assist with league administration, and ensure that our events continue to run smoothly and professionally.

Most importantly, the Jr. Board helps ensure that PRA can continue to grow while maintaining the competitive, respectful, and community-driven environment that our drivers expect.

We are excited to have these individuals stepping into leadership roles and contributing their time and effort to make PRA even better.

Please take a moment to congratulate our new Jr. Board Members the next time you see them in the server or on track.
